General Food Love

Food

I know you are all wondering what such a marvelous cat eats on a normal day. Like every lion, Alberto does not hunt, He gets fed by others. Preferably at any given moment of the day.

  1. Around 6 or 7 in the morning, he starts commanding for his humans to feed him his pre-breakfast. This consists mostly out of catisfactions. he gets around 5 to 10 catisfactions every morning.
  2. Around 8 in the morning his humans go down stairs with him, to open the breakfast buffet doors. he can eat as many chunks as he desires.
  3. This is the tricky part. The humans expect Alberto to drink from the same bowl as the dogs. I know... Disgusting. So Alberto needs to confiscate one of the glasses of water that have been put on the table, preferably those large ones with flowers in them.
  4. For the rest of the day, Alberto eats chunks as much as he pleases, with the occasional treat of some catisfactions.
  5. At 16.30 in the afternoon it is time for Alberto to notify his humans about the fact that it is almost 5 o'clock: dinnertime. at 5 sharp, his bowl needs to be filled with canned food. He. will. not. take. no. for. an answer.
  6. around 22.30 in the evening, it is time for Alberto to bring his humans upstairs. It is time for them to give him some more catisfactions and then preheat the bed for him so he can have a nice rest.
